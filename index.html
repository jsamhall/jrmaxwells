Proudly Serving Midcoast Maine since 1979

At J.R. Maxwell & Co., homemade is not just an advertising ploy- everything that we can, we cook in house. All of our salad dressings are made here, our soups are homemade, and our original chowder is famous up and down the seacoast. Our homemade sauces include Newberg, supreme, tartar, cocktail, and an Alfredo sauce to die for. Our seafood is fried in our own beer batter and our French onion Soup recipe is 40 years old. Our black angus steaks are all cut here at Maxwells. We even cook our own potato chips and make our own cheese sticks and onion rings. Our homemade deserts include chocolate cake, Key Lime pie, frozen Grasshopper and peanut butter pie, Carrot Cake, and Indian Pudding. We are an independent restaurant, not a chain. When you come in, we want you to become a regular customer. Yes, we are the place where everybody knows your name.

Visit the Boatbuilders Pub

Downstairs is the place where all the locals go, the Boat Builders Pub. Featuring a big screen TV, pool tables, and the atmosphere of the local pub, it is definitely a 'must stop' if you want to experience the 'City of Ships'.

Open Daily from Noon to 1:00AM with a happy hour from 3:30 to 6:00PM, come enjoy daily drink and food specials or something from the lunch and dinner menus. Every Sunday the Pool table is free to use from Noon to 5:00PM followed by a tournament at 6:00PM.
